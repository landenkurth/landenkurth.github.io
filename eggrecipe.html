scrambled eggs

here is the recipe for scrambled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs of butter
  • 2 tbs cream

method

  1. melted butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in the bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if your making an omelet)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the head( it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
